Flea

Michael Peter Balzary, known professionally as Flea, is an Australian-American musician and actor. He is best known as a founding member and the bassist of the rock band Red Hot Chili Peppers and has appeared on every album released by the band.

  • Born: October 16, 1962, Burwood, Australia
  • Height: 5′ 6″
  • Band: The Red Hot Chili Peppers
